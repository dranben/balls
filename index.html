💬 CHAT COMMANDS

  • !catchpokemon - Catch a wild Pokémon
  • !catchstats - Your trainer summary
  • !mypokemon - Link to your storage
  • !globalstats - Server-wide catch stats
  • !market - See all items for sale
  • !get [item] - Purchase an item (e.g. !get incense, !get master-ball, !get charm)
  • !bag - View your items & active buffs

⚔️ HOW BATTLES WORK

  • 1. Set a Pokémon in your Favorite Slot 1 (the red Battle Slot) on your trainer page — this is your fighter
  • 2. Type !battle @username to challenge someone
  • 3. They have 60 seconds to type !acceptbattle @you
  • 4. Winner is decided by each Pokémon's Base Stats + IVs — stronger Pokémon win more often but it's never guaranteed!
